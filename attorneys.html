Philip C. Barth III

Partner

Mr. Barth, grandson of the firm’s founder, is an experienced trial attorney who has tried cases for over twenty-five years. After graduating from the State University of New York at Buffalo Law School, Mr. Barth served as one of the most successful in-house trial attorneys at Fireman’s Fund Insurance Company. Since joining Barth Sullivan Behr in 1996, he has continued to amass an impressive record of trial and appellate successes. Mr. Barth has been an active member of the Defense Research Institute for many years and was nominated and approved for membership in the prestigious International Association of Defense Counsel (IADC) in 2004. He continues to litigate regularly, specializing in motor vehicle and premises liability.

Laurence D. Behr

Partner

Graduated from the State University of New York at Buffalo, B.A. cum laude (1974), and J.D. magna cum laude (1981). Following his two-year service as Confidential Law Clerk to the United States District Judge John T. Elfvin (Western District of New York), Mr. Behr forged a distinguished career as a successful personal injury defense trial and appellate lawyer. Mr. Behr held partnerships in two of Buffalo’s most respected insurance defense firms, the former Saperston & Day, P.C. and Lustig & Brown LLP, prior to joining Barth Sullivan Behr in 1999. He has tried over thirty personal injury cases to successful defense verdicts, and has published numerous leading articles on important and timely insurance law topics.

Douglas P. Hamberger

Senior Trial Attorney

Mr. Hamberger is a trial attorney with a wealth of experience, both civil and criminal, having tried cases for over thirty years. Following graduation from the University of Miami School of Law, Mr. Hamberger practiced law in South Florida before joining the Erie County District Attorney’s Office in Buffalo where he successfully tried in excess of five hundred criminal cases. Since joining Barth Sullivan Behr in 2007, he continues to try cases on a regular basis and concentrates his practice in the areas of motor vehicle liability, premises liability, toxic tort and labor law.

Syracuse Office

J. William Savage

Senior Trial Attorney

Mr. Savage is an experienced trial attorney who has tried cases throughout Upstate New York for fifteen years. Since joining Barth Sullivan Behr in 2010, he has achieved more than a dozen successful jury verdicts. Mr. Savage’s areas of concentration include automobile and premises liability, as well as pharmaceutical defense. In fact, Mr. Savage argued effectively before the Appellate Division, Third Department in Albany to change existing law concerning pharmacist liability in the case of Brumaghim v. Rite Aid, 94 AD3d 1391 (3rd Dept. 2012), which has been reviewed in several legal journals. Before embarking on his legal career, Mr. Savage served in the U. S. Navy on board the USS Midway and USS Reuben James.
